What is the BMI calculator?
The BMI calculator is a tool that determines if you are overweight, underweight, or have the right weight.
How to calculate BMI?
To calculate BMI you should use the following formula:
BMI = weight / (height in cm / 100)^2
Why use BMI calculator?
The BMI calculator is used in order to compute if you are overweight, underweight, or have the right weight.
